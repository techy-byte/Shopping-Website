Our Journey

From Bengaluru With Love —
Dressing India's Women

KHUSHI ENTERPRISES was born in 1998 from a simple dream — to make beautiful, high-quality Indian clothing accessible to every woman across India, without the hefty price tag.

What started as a small boutique in Rajajinagar, Bengaluru, has grown into a beloved women's fashion brand trusted by over 10,000 customers from Kanyakumari to Kashmir.

Every saree, kurti, and lehenga we offer is handpicked by our team of fashion experts who believe that style and affordability can coexist beautifully.
